Brake Failure Indicator

This project Brake Failure Indicator gives an early warning of brake while driving. This circuit provides a break failure indication which verifies the condition of the brake and display an audio as well as a visual indication for the person at every moment. Brake switch analyses the brake fluid and if the fluid level is low, the comparator activates the alarm and also gives a visual indication.

Gm-APD Cameras For 3-D Imaging

It describes cameras incorporating focal plane arrays of Geiger-mode avalanche photodiodes (Gm-APDs) that enable 3-D imaging in laser radar (LADAR) systems operating at wavelengths near 1.0 and 1.5 µm. Gm-APDs based on the InGaAsP material system achieve single-photon sensitivity at every pixel of the array and are hybridized to custom CMOS ROICs providing 0.25 ns timing resolution.

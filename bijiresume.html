1)Break Failure Indicator(Mini Project)

Project Description: This project Brake Failure Indicator gives an early warning of brake while driving. This circuit provides a break failure indication which verifies the condition of the brake and display an audio as well as a visual indication for the person at every moment. Brake switch analyses the brake fluid and if the fluid level is low, the comparator activates the alarm and also gives a visual indication. When the fluid of the brake is in a specified level, timer will get activated and gives the visual indication that the brake is in the good condition. The circuit will work only in vehicles with negative grounding. The test result proves that the user can identify the condition of the brake and thus limit the chances of accidents.
